Pēgvīle (Aitso dialect)

a-lit aim2 ye xom1. ua hut2 hia1-mun1 xit2 mun1 liu.

Alice exist at/in/on bank. and begin think_none due_to none have_to.

Alice was on the bank and was beginning to get bored of having nothing to do.

ua et2 yuo ik2 kak ta1 sa1 huok1 ta1 a ak1.

and one or two time see female sibling see 's book.

And once or twice she saw the book her sister was reading.

ua mun1 lek1 yuo huat1 zep1 ua hia1 'ze2 mun1 sui1' a.

and none picture/painting or each_other talk and think 'this none use' ah.

But there were no pictures or conversations; she thought 'this is of no use.'

1. Alice in wonderland

First paragraph:

alic-∅ sá-homá woghit-i cájau ṣoma-ḍi hína ṣíkah-azená hem.

Alice-NOM female-GEN sibling-POST　along_with　bank-POST at/in/on　sit-3F.PST.PTCP PFV

Alice was sitting sitting by her sister on the bank.

ṣiṇṇa, baukká koli-dhel cákíka-kátá hem.

but gradually tiredness-ACC　make_arrive-3N.PST.PTCP PFV

However, she was gradually getting tired.

lási-homá káṇa-∅ jo ríka-homá káṇa-ḍi hína ká-∅ aghauṭa-dhel nána sá-homá woghit-∅ ák-aze aḍuhazo-zená hem.

one-GEN time-ZERO　or　two-GEN time-POST at/in/on she-NOM　book-ACC　REL　female-GEN sibling-NOM　read-3F.REAL　see-3F.PST.PTCP PFV

Once or twice she saw the book that her sister was reading.

ṣiṇṇa, legha-∅ jo cepatepa-∅ aghauṭa-ḍi hína ám hem-akátá hem.

but picture/painting-ZERO or conversation-NOM　book-POST　at/in/on not exist-3N.PST.PTCP PFV

But there weren't pictures or conversations in the book.

"há, ko-dhel nána-ḍi hína suj-úḷ-∅ heyá-káṣlo?"

"oh this-ACC　what-POST　at/in/on　use-INF-NOM　think-3N.FUT.PTCP(=I_wonder...)」

"Well then, I wonder how this thing is to be used."

alic-∅ ko-ḍi ema heyá-zená hem.

　Alice-NOM　this-POST [object] think-3F.PST.PTCP PFV

Alice thought.

"legha-∅ jo cepatepa-∅ ám hem-úḷ-∅ ema."

"picture/painting-ZERO　or　conversation-NOM not exist-INF-POST　[disbelief]"

"unbelievably, there aren't any pictures or conversations."

Note

  • Even though the original text is "beginning to get very tired of", it is mistranslated here as "beginning to get very tired from".
  • Since nouns following a numeral must be in singular, it is ríkahomá káṇaḍi hína, not *ríkahomá káṇadhína.
